Artifacts associated with velocity discontinuities

First order velocity discontinuities totally reflect rays with ray parameters in the range covered by the velocity jump. The determination of the affected ray parameters is straight forward and independent of the depth sampling, but the prediction of the epicentral distance at which the reflected rays arrive at the surface is not.
