Supporting Routines

TTBOX uses several routines which I have written outside the TTBOX context, and most of them were written before I even started the development of TTBOX. So these routines are not really part of the TTBOX project.

On the other hand, TTBOX does not work without these routines, so the distribution has to include them. These Routines are therefore included in the "support" subdirectory of the TTBOX directory.

The TTBOX online documentation does not provide any help for these routines, but you may read the help lines of the m-files themselves.
